# BrowserBase Data Platform North Star

## Mission
Build a production-grade data platform that makes trusted, self-serve analytics the default and supports future agentic/AI workflows.

## Strategic Outcomes
1. Build the foundations.
- Design, build, and maintain production-grade pipelines and analytics models.
- Establish standards for data quality, reliability, and documentation.
- Lay the groundwork for AI-assisted workflows.

2. Define and own the metrics layer.
- Govern core business metrics, definitions, and instrumentation standards.
- Align Growth, Product, Engineering, Ops, and Finance on sources of truth.
- Ensure metrics are trusted, understood, and consistently used.

3. Enable self-serve analytics.
- Reduce ad-hoc request load via governed tools and metadata.
- Enable teams to answer their own questions with confidence.
- Promote responsible, policy-compliant AI analytics usage.

4. Scale the data function.
- After platform foundations are stable, define hiring and org design roadmap.
- Evolve responsibilities toward formal Head of Data scope.

## Operating Principles
- Trust over speed when they conflict.
- Certified metrics over bespoke calculations.
- Metadata first, SQL second.
- Policy and auditability by default.
- Reusable systems over one-off reporting.
